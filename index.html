HR Warning Scheme ‚ö†Ô∏è

HRs have their own warnings separate from workers. If you are caught breaking any HR regulations you will be warned.

Verbal warnings: These warnings are simply a verbal reminder and are sent to you via a warning bot. Three verbal warnings accumulate to one official warning.

Official Warnings: After receiving an official warning, you will be informed of what you did wrong. After three official warnings, you may be demoted by one rank for one month.
